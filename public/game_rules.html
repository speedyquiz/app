SPEEDQUIZZ

Playing Rules

 

Eligibility:

    • Players / Competitors must be at least 18 years old to participate.
    • Residents of countries where online quiz participation and Games of Skill for potential reward is legal, are eligible to enter.
    • Players / Competitors must be registered Subscribers with completed profiles to use the platform
    • Employees of SPEEDQUIZZ and their immediate family members are not eligible to receive any rewards or prizes

 

Gameplay Overview:

    • SPEEDQUIZZ is a timed quiz app where players compete against each other to answer a series of quiz questions to achieve the maximum score of 100% in the quickest time possible.
    • Each game consists of a fixed number of questions, varying between 10 to 15 questions per game.
    • A new quiz game, featuring different categories, is launched every 2 minutes, 24 hours a day, ensuring continuous gameplay.

 

Points:

    • Players earn points for each correct answer.
    • The total score is calculated as a total of correct answers and/or a percentage based on the number of correct answers out of the total questions.
    • Achieving certain Points Levels will entitle the Player to utilize / redeem those points for prizes or access to special games at SPEEDQUIZZ’s  discretion.

 

Timed Gameplay:

    • Each quiz game has a predetermined time limit for answering questions, typically ranging from 60 to 90 seconds.
    • In the event of unanswered questions, they will be marked as incorrect.
    • All players competing in the same “quiz” will have the same amount of time to answer the questions and complete the game.
    • There may be a latency between devices of multiple players, so start times may vary slightly.
    • Once confirmed to play, the player will be notified via a countdown and then a 5 second period to initiate play.

 

Competition and Prizes:

    • Players compete against each other in real-time to achieve the highest score within the shortest time.
    • Prizes are awarded to player(s) who achieve the maximum score of 100% in the shortest time.

 

Prize Distribution:

    • Cash prizes are deposited directly into the winners' designated bank accounts or payment wallets within a specified timeframe.
    • E-Gift prizes are delivered to the winners' registered addresses via email or via text sms
    • In the event of more than one player achieving the exact winning time then the reward will be either split evenly between the Players or the equal prize value to be allocated to each Player or a Points allocation given to each Player equivalent to the Split Value – at the discretion of SPEEDQUIZZ
    • SPEEDQUIZZ will always endeavour to deliver all rewards and prizes within 24-48 hours

 

Fair Play and Integrity:

    • Cheating, use of external aids, or any form of unfair practices are strictly prohibited.
    • The quiz app employs measures to detect and prevent cheating, including but not limited to, time-stamping responses, randomizing question orders, and monitoring player activity.
    • Any attempts to manipulate the game or exploit vulnerabilities will result in disqualification and potential legal action.

 

NOTE: Each quiz game is designed to challenge all players / competitors, so time allocated to complete the quiz is commensurate with perceived difficulty and actual physicality. Hence SPEEDQUIZZ will not allow a Player / Competitor to achieve an overall “Winner Status” if the game is completed with 100% correct answers in a time sooner than 30% of the allocated time – example: A game is completed with 10/10 questions correct in 15 seconds. This is designed to eliminate “Bashers” who are Players that just flick through each question without reading or observing the images and just pushing any answer option to complete the game in the fastest time.

User Conduct:

    • Players are expected to maintain sportsmanship and respectful conduct towards other participants.
    • Offensive language, harassment, or any form of inappropriate behaviour will not be tolerated and may result in immediate disqualification and suspension from the app.

 

Privacy and Data Security:

    • The quiz app adheres to strict privacy policies to safeguard user data and information.
    • Personal information collected during registration and gameplay is used solely for the purpose of administering the contest and awarding prizes.

Modification of Rules:

    • The quiz app reserves the right to modify contest rules, game mechanics, prize structures, or any other aspect of the competition at any time without prior notice.
    • Contestants will be notified of any changes via the app's official communication channels.

 

Legal Compliance:

    • Participation in SPEEDQUIZZ constitutes acceptance of these contest rules and compliance with all applicable laws and regulations.
    • SPEEDQUIZZ reserves the right to disqualify contestants who violate the rules or fail to comply with legal requirements.

 

Contact Information:

    • For inquiries, feedback, or assistance regarding SPEEDQUIZZ, contestants can contact customer support through the app's designated channels.

By participating in SPEEDQUIZZ contestants acknowledge and agree to abide by the aforementioned rules and regulations governing the contest. Good luck and may the best quizzer win!
